Be a volunteer

Creatives

Unleash your creativity in a space where imagination meets innovation. Whether you're passionate about photography, video production, or graphic design, we've got the tools and inspiration to help you bring your ideas to life. Capture stunning visuals with our photo and video shooting resources, and take your editing skills to the next level with professional-grade tools. From concept to creation, you're in control of the entire storytelling process. Design eye-catching graphics that speak volumes. Whether it’s for digital marketing, social media, or branding, your visual content will leave a lasting impression.

Worship

Create an inviting atmosphere filled with heartfelt praise and worship. Whether you sing, play an instrument, or simply have a passion for leading others into meaningful worship experiences, this is your space to grow and serve. Join a community that values authenticity, reverence, and creativity in worship.

Production

Be part of the team that helps bring church to life by producing our live services and events. From managing sound and lighting to running screens, cameras, and other technical elements, you'll play a key role in creating a smooth and impactful experience for everyone attending—both in person and online. Whether you're experienced or just eager to learn, there’s a place for you to grow and serve behind the scenes.

Teachers

Help raise up the next generation of world-changers by creating a fun, safe, and engaging environment for children. As a teacher, you’ll prepare the kids' space, warmly welcome children and their families, and build meaningful connections with parents. You’ll play a vital role in teaching biblical truths, leading activities, and supporting our kids' programs to make each weekend impactful and memorable.

Usher

Create a warm and welcoming environment by giving new people the VIP experience from the moment they arrive. As an usher, you’ll prepare and distribute welcome gift bags during the service, help guests feel at home, and connect with them personally after the service. Your role continues throughout the week by following up and building relationships that help newcomers feel seen, valued, and encouraged to get connected.
