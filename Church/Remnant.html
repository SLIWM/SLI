REMNANT

a Remnant is a generation of young believers who choose to stand firm in faith and purpose. Rooted in the biblical meaning of a remnant—a faithful few who remain true to God—we aim to live a life that leaves a legacy of faith for the next generation. We are the remnant that leaves a remnant, carrying God’s light forward for others to follow.
