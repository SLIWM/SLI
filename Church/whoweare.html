Who we are

OUR STORY

Salt and Light s a God-centered, gospel-driven Christian mission organization committed to glorifying God and serving people through the two Cs—Community and Church—as disciples of Christ Jesus. Rooted in the Reformed faith, we seek to live as the salt and light of the world—loving God, loving others, and reflecting Christ in our daily lives. We are a family in Christ, growing together in grace and walking together in faith.

The ministry began in 2005, when Missionary Sungwon Lee started sponsoring a student he met among the railway settlers in San Pedro, Laguna as part of a community outreach. By the will of God, church ministry began in 2009 with volunteers and the families we served. Through the grace of God, the ministry continues to grow—all for His glory.

Where We're Going

Our vision statement is simple: to know God and make him famous. Knowing God doesn't just mean knowing about God—it means having a real, intimate relationship with him. We desire to reach people across the Philippines and across the world with the good news of Jesus, helping them to encounter him in a real, authentic way. We're passionate about making God famous, and we believe we're called to plant churches in major cities across the Philippines, Asia, and the world—churches marked by passionate worship, inspired creativity, a hunger for God's presence, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

What We Value

1. Worship

Worshiping our Almighty God not only in gathered services but also through the daily journey of faith. We seek and experience God’s presence as we worship Him in spirit and truth.

2. Discipleship

Walking in faith and obedience. We grow as disciples of Jesus Christ and help others become His disciples.

3. Compassion & Service

Loving others in practical and holistic ways. We care for the vulnerable and serve our neighborhoods with the love of Christ.

4. Mission

Fulfilling the Great Commission. We proclaim the gospel, equip believers, and make disciples locally and globally.

5. Family in Christ

Dreaming together and growing together—in Christ and for Christ. We are a spiritual family encouraging one another, united in grace, and walking together in the Lord.
